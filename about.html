Company Overview

Lucreskill Tech is an integrative and interactive eLearning solution provider, having development centers in Pune and New Delhi, India, established in 2007. We create and customize learning methodologies and techniques, and combine them with the best practices, to yield enhanced digital learning experiences for millennial learners.

With 800 + projects over a decade of our existence, we have sustained an annual growth rate of 100%, establishing our potential as a dynamic organization.

Our high delivery standards make us eLearning partners to numerous global organizations of varied industries and scales. Our clientele includes private and public sector enterprises, corporates, universities, institutes, schools, and eLearning companies, spread across Asia, Europe, United Kingdom, and the United States.

At Lucreskill Tech, we work on the cornerstones of value, trust, commitment, and creativity. From paying attention to the finer details of our clients’ requirements to ensuring long-standing relationships with them, we provide constant support at every stage of our client’s journey and make learning interesting for all.
