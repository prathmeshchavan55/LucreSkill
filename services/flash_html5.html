Flash To HTML5 Conversion

Converting your old Flash-based courses to HTML5 mobile responsive elearning courses

When an organization want to convert their Flash based courses to HTML5, the major constrain will be the budget. Migration is an overhead cost to the organization, especially if the courses are in high number. Considering the budget and the timeline, here are different approaches we follow for converting from Flash to HTML5.

Our Flash to HTML5 conversion services includes:

Mobile friendliness

Flash is incompatible with mobile devices such as iOS-based Apple devices, laptops, smartphones, iPhones, and iPad, while HTML5 is fully mobile compatible.

Better SEO strategies

Flash is not friendly with SEO; it requires Adobe Flash Player plug-in and higher processing power to operate.

Less Power consumption

If we compare both Flash and HTML5 and Flash, HTML5 consumes less processing power, runs faster than Flash.

Security concerns

Flash has several security issues and flaws that make it vulnerable. Hackers find it easier to attack Flash websites and games.
